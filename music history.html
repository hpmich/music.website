Ancient Music (3000 BCE - 500 CE):

- Mesopotamia: instruments like lyres and flutes

- Ancient Greece: birthplace of Western music, with modes and scales

- Rome: adopted Greek music, added instruments like trumpets

Medieval Music (500 - 1450):

- Gregorian chant, plainsong, and troubadours

- Emergence of polyphony and harmony

Renaissance Music (1450 - 1600):

- Revival of ancient Greek and Roman music

- Development of instruments like lutes and violins

Baroque Music (1600 - 1750):

- Ornate, complex compositions - Famous composers: Bach, Handel, Vivaldi

Classical Music (1750 - 1820):

- Balanced, structured compositions - Famous composers: Haydn, Mozart, Beethoven

20th Century Music (1910 - 2000):

- Jazz, blues, and swing - Rock 'n' roll, pop, and folk - Electronic, hip-hop, and rap

21st Century Music (2000 - present):

- Digital music and streaming - Diverse genres and global influences
